About Us

New Age Engineering Precast boundary wall is a high quality precast concrete walling system featuring natural looking stone textures and architectural form finishes on both sides of the wall. Our attention to detail and refusal to cut corners, results in long lasting and highly durable fencing structures.

Our walls provide a quieter and more aesthetically pleasing environment for residential, commercial and industrial applications.

New Age Engineering Precast is the first company to introduce form texture appearance on both sides of the wall. Connec walls come with a Uni-Cast System wherein the column and panel are cast as one integrated unit. We also the first one to introduce the Static footing support system, which provides safe secure and immediate support to the panels after being installed.

We at New Age Engineering offer you the most advanced precast forming system to manufacture these precast walls.

PRECAST WALL PRODUCT
The New Age Precast Walling System is a highly effcient wall design, manufactured vertically with the column and panel integrally cast as a single wall section. New Age Walls are supported with structural steel support columns embedded in caissons located sixteen feet on-center. The walls are available in heights from six to twelve feet.
